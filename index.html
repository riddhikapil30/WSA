OUR PROGRAM

OUR MISSION
Our mission is to serve the animals that are needy and need a helping hand for a better life
WHAT WE DO
WSA provides shelter for sick, old and abandoned dogs, cats, cows and every other animal. They are not chained or caged, and have enough space to walk, run and play. There are only a few cages in the shelter for those undergoing treatment.
